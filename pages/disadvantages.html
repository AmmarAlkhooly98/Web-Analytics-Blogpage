Disadvantages of Web Analytics

here's the reference for this article ©

Generally, there are very few cons to using web analytics. However, there are a couple of things to mention.
Below is a list of the cons of data analytics:

Dependence on JavaScript and Cookies

JavaScript can reveal the pages the user's are looking at, the browser they use, etc., while cookies are responsible for storing all of this information and determining visitor uniqueness.

Page Performance



Visitors may experience a change in the download time of your site, as the JavaScript code needed to track your pages is never zero-weight. Vendors that tout otherwise are incorrect.

Privacy Concerns



Privacy is important for consumers. Issues abound concerning the types of data collected, how they are used and where they are stored. that's why most vendors issue a privacy policy notifying users of data collection procedures.
